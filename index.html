LEADING BIKE MANUFACTURERS IN INDIA



Honda was found by SOICHIRO HONDA and was established in 1937, the first complete motorcycle with 
engine was build by Honda


The first Royal Enfield motorcycle was built in 1901. The Enfield Cycle Company is responsible for
the design and original production of the Royal Enfield Bullet.


KTM AG (Kraftfahrzeug Trunkenpolz Mattighofen, formerly KTM Sportmotorcycle AG) is an 
Austrian motorcycle, bicycle and sports car manufacturer 


Yamaha corporation in 1887 used to manufacture pianos, after 1955 it started its first bike 125cc 


Bajaj Group is an Indian multinational conglomerate founded by Jamnalal Bajaj in Mumbai in 1926.


Hero MotoCorp Limited, formerly Hero Honda, is an Indian multinational motorcycle manufacturer 
headquartered in New Delhi. The company is one of the largest two-wheeler manufacturers in the world


Suzuki manufactures automobiles, motorcycles, all-terrain vehicles (ATVs), outboard marine engines, 
wheelchairs and a variety of other small internal combustion engines.


Piaggio is an Italian motor vehicle manufacturer, which produces a range of two-wheeled motor vehicles
and compact commercial vehicles 


Bayerische Motoren Werke AG, abbreviated as BMW is a German multinational manufacturer of performance 
luxury vehicles and motorcycles headquartered in Munich, Bavaria, Germany.
